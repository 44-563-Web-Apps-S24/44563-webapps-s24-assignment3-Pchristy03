Celestia Peach the Panda

Celestia Peach the Panda is a gentle and enchanting creature with peach and white fur. Known for her nurturing spirit, she tends to the Bamboo Grove Glade, bringing magic and vibrancy to the surroundings. With celestial eyes and a flower crown, Celestia is a symbol of harmony in her magical forest home.
